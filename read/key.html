Inflection formatting

This document exists to give a sample of formatting that represents inflections. Gender options are masculine, feminine, neuter, and no gender. Verb aspect options are perfective, imperfective, and no aspect.

                                    Masculine       Feminine       Neuter       No gender
No aspect masculine feminine neuter none
Imperfective aspect masculine feminine neuter none
Perfective aspect masculine feminine neuter none

Paradigm of the verb "to call" in the first-person singular active indicative and as an active participle in the nominative singular masculine:

Tense             Finite verb (V-*AI-1S)       Participle (V-*AP-NSM)
Present I call calling
Imperfect I called —
Aorist I called calling
Future I will call going to call
Perfect I have called having called
Pluperfect I had called —
